Interval Timer

  • Click on 'Create' and give your interval a title
  • Add interval description, and choose duration
  • Click on 'Add Interval' to add additional intervals
  • Once you have added all of your intervals, click 'Save'
  • Click on 'Saved' to access intervals that you have created
  • Choose an interval and click 'Start' to get your HIIT on!
